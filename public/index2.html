Introduction

League 6m (or l6m for short) is a Starcraft 2 Custom league. The big difference between l6m and the battle.net ladder, is the map pool. l6m only features maps of which the bases contains 6 minerals and one high yield gas (shorthand 6m1hyg).

Read more in-depth about this in the teamliquid thread Breadth of gameplay.
